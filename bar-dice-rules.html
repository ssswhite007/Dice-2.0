Hand Name Description Score
Five of a Kind All five dice show the same number. 100 Points
Four of a Kind Four dice show the same number. 80 Points
Full House A combination of Three of a Kind and a Pair (e.g., three 3's and two 4's). 60 Points
Straight Five consecutive numbers (e.g., 1-2-3-4-5 or 2-3-4-5-6). 50 Points
Three of a Kind Three dice show the same number. 40 Points
Two Pair Two different pairs (e.g., two 2's and two 5's). 20 Points
One Pair Two dice show the same number. 10 Points
Chance Any combination not fitting the above categories; score is the sum of all dice. Sum of Dice
